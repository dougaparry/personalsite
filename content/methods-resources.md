# Research Methods Resources

I keep this list updated with links to useful blogs and articles relevant to research methods in computational social science. For other resources see the follow pages: [R-resources](/r-resources/), [CSS-resources](/css-resources/).

* [Simply Statistics](https://simplystatistics.org) - a blog on data science, analysis, and statistics
